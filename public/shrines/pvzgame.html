Plants vs Zombies (abbreviated pvz), is a tower defense/strategy type of game first published by Popcan in 2009! The game takes place in the zombie apocalypse and those zombies want to invade your house and eat your brains (yummy) What do you do? defend your home... with plans...! Weird looking plants manufactured by Bloom & Doom, but it doesn't matter, they're defending your home so be grateful!

Gameplay



You will be given seed packets during the game, each one with a price in sun, the game's main currency which falls from the sky, you can also get them with the sunflower of course! You can't just bring all the plants with you tho there's a slot limit for now.
Many plants have different abilities and stats that will help you through the 5 game areas. Zombies aren't left behind, the hordes will include tougher zombies with other abilities, but that's what makes it an strategy game.
Zombies will appear in different lanes on your yard, if they get you... you still have your lawnmower to run them over but from then... start planting.

PvZ also includes minigames, Crazy Dave's Twiddydinkies (aka Dave's shop) and game modes, but those come while you are playing.
