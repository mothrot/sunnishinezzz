welcome to SapphicRing a webring made for those who identify as sapphics on the web, brought to you thanks to Webringu by Petrapixel

Sapphic is an umbrella term for women (and recently women-aligned and non-binary identities) who are attracted to women or in a relationship with another woman, which includes lesbians, bisexuals, queer, etc.

The origin for the term sapphic comes from the name of the Greek poetess Sappho, who expressed her love for women in her works. The term lesbian shares a symilar origin, inspired by Sappho's birth place, Lesbos.
